  <section id="about">
    <h2>Our Mission</h2>
    <p>
      At <strong>Pedal4Water</strong>, we’re revolutionizing how rural communities access clean water by transforming bicycles into life-saving tools. Our innovative bike attachments help families pump, carry, and deliver clean water—faster and easier. We're starting in Kenya, where millions walk miles daily just to access unsafe water.
    </p>
  </section>

  <section id="how">
    <h2>How It Works</h2>
    <p><strong>Step 1:</strong> 🚴 Custom Bike Builds – We retrofit local bikes with jerry can holders and pump attachments.</p>
    <p><strong>Step 2:</strong> 💧 Pump & Transport Water – Pedaling powers a pump or allows larger water loads to be carried.</p>
    <p><strong>Step 3:</strong> 🌍 Deliver Impact – Each water bike helps reduce time spent fetching water by 70%.</p>
  </section>
